R-VAE User study

You are now invited to play with a rhythmic interface called R-VAE.

In R-VAE, a rhythmic space is represented by an area in your web browser where you can play with rhythms.

In the R-VAE rhythmic space you can:

  • Click to retrieve a rhythmic pattern
  • Drag the mouse to interpolate between patterns in the space

R-VAE knobs

The interface has four knobs:

  • Threshold will modify the number of onsets retrieved
  • Noise will add variability to the position of the mouse
  • Tempo controls the speed of the sequence
  • Volume controls the level of the drums

R-VAE buttons

The interface has four buttons:

  • Kk, Sn, and Hh will mute individual kick, snare, and hihats
  • Those buttons are also mapped to the keyboard shortcuts Q, W, and E
  • Mute all drums by pressing All or R
  • Rhythmic trajectories can be cleared by pressing C
